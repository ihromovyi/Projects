# E-commerce Web Analytics — Why a Traffic Surge Didn't Grow Revenue

5 months of Google Merchandise Store data (Aug–Dec 2016) analysed in Tableau: funnel, channels, visitors and cohorts.

**Question:** as holiday traffic grew, did revenue grow with it?

**Key finding:** a paid **Social** campaign brought a wave of low-converting traffic from Asian markets (Vietnam, India, Thailand) — excluding the US, revenue was near zero. The money is concentrated in **US + returning visitors**.

**Recommendations:** cut the vanity-traffic campaign; reinvest in US channels (Direct, Organic Search); fix the **View → Cart** drop-off (only 31% advance); build a retention play.
